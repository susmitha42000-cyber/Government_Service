Our Mission

The National Government Portal brings public services, official information, applications, notices, and citizen support into one reliable digital access point.

It is designed to reduce repeated office visits, improve service visibility, and help citizens complete essential government tasks with clearer guidance and better tracking.

  • Single-window access for certificates, licenses, welfare schemes, departments, notices, and tracking.
  • Citizen-first design with clear application steps, service categories, and support pathways.
  • Secure handling of public-service interactions using transparent and accountable digital processes.

Portal Priorities

  • Accessibility for citizens across devices, locations, and service needs.
  • Faster turnaround through online submission and application status updates.
  • Data protection practices that support secure access to government services.
  • Better communication through notices, updates, and department information.
