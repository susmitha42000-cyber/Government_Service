Before Contacting a Department

  • Keep application number, identity proof, and relevant documents ready.
  • Check public notices for deadlines, office timings, tenders, and recruitment updates.
  • Use official contact channels and avoid sharing sensitive information through unofficial sources.
  • Track applications online before raising a follow-up request with the department.

Citizen Coordination

Departments work together across services. For example, a welfare application may need identity verification, income records, bank details, and department approval.

The portal helps citizens identify the right department and reduces confusion by grouping related services, notices, and tracking options in one place.
